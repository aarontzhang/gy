## Meta-notes

- **Wedges that match your edge**: creator/Gen Z products, university-native distribution, California-specific plays (wildfire, pools, med spa, real estate), AI-native versions of boring industries you can operate from a laptop, anything that lives inside Shopify/Discord/Slack/TikTok.
- **Wedges to be suspicious of**: anything requiring 20 years of relationships (freight, oil & gas, defense primes), anything that needs boomer trust you can't earn at 21 (big enterprise security, institutional finance), anything with a 5-year regulatory approval cycle before revenue.
- **The pattern**: almost every billion-dollar version ends up in one of three shapes — *AI-operated boring business* (rollups, operator plays), *AI-native back office for a fragmented class of workers* (solo economy, SMBs), or *AI-native consumer product with viral Gen Z distribution* (merch, tax, screen time, pet, content).
